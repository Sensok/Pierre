Jordan's Goulash
--------------------

Ingredients
--------------------

1 Bag Cool Ranch Doritos
1 LBS Ground Beef
1 Onion
2 TBS Chili Powder
1 TBS Onion Powder
Sour Cream
Cheese
Mild Salsa

Directions
--------------------

Cook meat with spices and onion.
Place over Doritos
Add massive amounts of cheese!
Bake for 3-5 min on BROIL to melt cheese
Add sour cream and salsa to taste
ENJOY!
